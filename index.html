Thoroughbred Retirement Foundation

Improving their lives after racing.

TRF strives to prevent future cruelty towards horses by educating the community on responsible equine stewardship.

TRF provides rescue, rehabilitation and permanent placement to abused, neglected or at risk equines. Life long sanctuary is provided to those equines unable to find permanent homes; and education, advocacy and equine support is offered throughout the community.
